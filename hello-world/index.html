Hello World from 22.3193° 114.1694°! Thanks for clicking in my personal website based on Hexo!

The Kick-off

This is the first post of my blog. The reasons I wanted to open a personal website were

  1. to be inspired by some of my friends
  2. to record and summarize my learning materials on a comprehensive platform
  3. to help serve as a demonstration of the projects I have done
  4. Perhaps also as a record of my life

Having a personal website was actually one of my goals when I first took the “HTML, CSS, Javascript” course in university. Back then I had absolutely no background in web development or programming, so I wanted a platform that would allow me to connect with other learners, communicate on specific issues, and consolidate what we had learned. I believe it would be a good starting point.

Theme: Per Aspera Ad Astra

There is a Latin proverb dating back 2000 years that means “Through Adversity to the Stars” or “Through Difficulty to the Stars”. Whenever an obstacle appears that blocks the way forward, that obstacle actually serves an important function.

This is an inspiring quote that reminds me to keep moving forward. The truth is, the hardest thing about stopping moving forward is the lack of self-efficacy in the face of a challenge and being afraid to try. Frankly, the only worst thing is when you try something and you don’t like it, but you still get some extra sense of accomplishment from the experience.

I am a person who enjoys topics related to the universe, astrophysics, and technology. I hope to use this quote to encourage myself to explore more in these areas.

The end

I hope you enjoyed reading this post! It is great to e-meet you here, no matter which corner of the World you are in. I am looking forward to sharing more with you in the future.
